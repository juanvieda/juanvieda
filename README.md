### 🧭 About me

Operations Analytics leader with **6+ years** across data analytics, finance, and BI. I sit at the intersection of **operations, technology, and AI** — architecting data pipelines, automating workflows, integrating GPS/IoT fleet data across platforms, and building dashboards that give leadership real-time visibility into what actually matters.

Currently leading the **Operations Analytics function at Zonar Systems** — a team I largely built from the ground up. My scope spans **sales ops, churn analysis, financial reporting, API integrations, and AI-powered automation**.

> AI isn't a buzzword for me. It's a tool I use every day.

---

### 🛠️ What I do

- 🏗️ **Design end-to-end data architectures** across Sales, Customer Success, and Operations
- 🤖 **Drive AI & automation** with n8n, Claude, and custom API integrations
- 📊 **Architect real-time dashboards** for bookings, pipeline, churn risk, and key account health
- 🔌 **Build pipelines** connecting GPS/IoT fleet data, CRM platforms, and internal systems
- 🎯 **Predictive retention** — flag at-risk accounts before they churn
- 📈 **Monthly Operating Review (MOR)** — board-level reporting with consistency across data sources
- 🤝 **Translate data into decisions** with Sales, Finance, Product, and Engineering teams
